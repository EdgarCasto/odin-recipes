Pitta Bread Pizza


Description:

Simple, quick and affordable pizza to satiate a craving or get a quick meal.
Can be paired with many ingredients, so you decide if you want a protein-filled meal, or just a tasty snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Slice of Pitta Bread
  • Tomato Sauce (with Garlic and Onion is also an option)
  • Grated Cheese
  • Any Toppings of your choice (Tuna and Salame are personal favourites)

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 200ºC (392ºF).
  2. Spread tomate sauce over the pitta bread until the entire surface is evenly covered.
  3. Spread the grated cheese over the tomato sauce.
  4. Place your toppings of choice over the grated cheese.
  5. Put the assembled pizza in the oven and let it cook for 5-6 minutes, or until the bread is crispy.
